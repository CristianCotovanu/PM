LCD:
    - GND <-> GND
    - VCC <-> 5V
    - SDA <-> A4
    - SCL <-> A5

BUZZER:
    - black <-> GND
    - red   <-> 12

TEMP & HUMIDITY SENSOR:
    - '-'        <-> GND
    - middle pin <-> 5V
    - S          <-> 4

FLAME SENSOR:
    - GND   <-> GND
    - VCC   <-> 5V
    - D0    <-> A0

GAS SENSOR:
    - GND   <-> GND
    - VCC   <-> 5V
    - A0    <-> A1

WIFI MODULE:
    - GND   <-> GND
    - VCC   <-> 5V
    - A0    <-> A1
